# Video Script Skill

Use this skill to turn a topic, product, workflow, or real project into a short-video script and production package.

## Workflow

1. Identify platform, length, audience, and source material.
2. Choose structure: demo, story, tutorial, teardown, problem-solution, or recap.
3. Write hook, beat sheet, shot list, voiceover, captions, and CTA.
4. Mark required assets and missing proof.
5. Use preview-first production when a video will be reviewed by timecode before final rendering.
6. Keep copyrighted and private media out of public artifacts.

## Outputs

- Title options.
- Timeline script.
- Shot list.
- Voiceover.
- Caption package.
- Asset checklist.
- Verification checklist.
- Preview review notes and production state labels.

## Do Not

- Do not invent screenshots, UI states, metrics, or user results.
- Do not include private footage, licensed music, or copyrighted media in public repos.
- Do not call a rendered MP4 publish-ready until timecode review, QA, and final editor polish gates are complete.
